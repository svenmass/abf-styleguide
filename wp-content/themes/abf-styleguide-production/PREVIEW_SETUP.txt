=== BLOCK PREVIEW SETUP ===

Um professionelle Block-Previews zu erstellen:

1. Erstelle Screenshots der Blöcke (empfohlene Größe: 1200x800px)
2. Speichere sie als PNG-Dateien in: assets/images/block-previews/
3. Verwende folgende Dateinamen:

Block: headline - Preview: assets/images/block-previews/headline.png
Block: hero - Preview: assets/images/block-previews/hero.png
Block: parallax-content - Preview: assets/images/block-previews/parallax-content.png
Block: parallax-element - Preview: assets/images/block-previews/parallax-element.png
Block: parallax-grid - Preview: assets/images/block-previews/parallax-grid.png
Block: styleguide-akkordeon - Preview: assets/images/block-previews/styleguide-akkordeon.png
Block: styleguide-bild-text - Preview: assets/images/block-previews/styleguide-bild-text.png
Block: styleguide-einzelbild - Preview: assets/images/block-previews/styleguide-einzelbild.png
Block: styleguide-grid - Preview: assets/images/block-previews/styleguide-grid.png
Block: styleguide-masonry - Preview: assets/images/block-previews/styleguide-masonry.png
Block: styleguide-posts - Preview: assets/images/block-previews/styleguide-posts.png
Block: styleguide-similar - Preview: assets/images/block-previews/styleguide-similar.png
Block: styleguide-text-element - Preview: assets/images/block-previews/styleguide-text-element.png
Block: styleguide-trennlinie - Preview: assets/images/block-previews/styleguide-trennlinie.png
Block: text-block - Preview: assets/images/block-previews/text-block.png

4. Die Previews werden automatisch im Block-Editor angezeigt

TIPPS FÜR GUTE PREVIEWS:
- Verwende realistische Inhalte
- Zeige typische Anwendungsfälle
- Nutze einheitliches Styling
- Optimiere für Webansicht (JPG/PNG < 200KB)

THEME SCREENSHOT:
Erstelle auch ein screenshot.png (1200x900px) für das Theme-Verzeichnis.
